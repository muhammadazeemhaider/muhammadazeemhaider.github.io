Hi, I'm Azeem. I am a computer scientist who enjoys researching how humans operate when it comes to language and their cognitive abilities. I graduated from Habib university in 2024 with a BS (Honors) in computer science and a minor in electrical and computer engineering.

My research currently focuses on cognition and linguistics, more specifically, how visual context plays a role in the way people perceive concepts.

Research Interests

This section focuses on my research interests in the domain of pure linguistics, computational linguistics, and natural language processing.

  • Exploring how words and phrases are structured and stored in the brain, and investigating the cognitive processes involved in their recall.
  • Investigating the impact of language structure on the formation and organization of concepts in the brain.
  • Studying multilingualism, language acquisition, and language structure in the brain through neurobiological methods and functional MRIs.
  • Language acquisition in infants within multilingual environments and the impact of these environments.

Previous Research Work
